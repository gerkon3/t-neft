Oil refinery in the Town of Aldan

Project Objective:
Production of petroleum products to meet the needs of enterprises and the population of the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia) in full.

Products characteristics:
Class 5 gasoline, class 5 diesel fuel, JET-A fuel (TS-1), bitumen, boiler and marine fuel, liquefied gases C3-C4, fuel gas, sulfur.

The main stages of the project:
Design, construction, operation.

Location:
Aldan, Aldan district, Republic of Sakha (Yakutia), Russia.

Availability of raw materials:
Oil supply in the amount of 2 million tons from ESPO (OPS-17, Aldan).

Markets:
The Republic of Sakha (Yakutia) and adjacent regions of the Russian Federation.

Annual production volume:
1,9 million tons of petroleum products.

Project Duration:
Output to design capacity - 4 years from the beginning of the design.

Exploration and development of the West Anabar oil field.

Project Objective:
Exploration and development of a promising oil field in the Arctic zone of the Russian Federation.

Products characteristics:
Crude oil, natural gas, gas condensate.

The main stages of the project:
Geological exploration, production drilling, field construction, construction of supply pipelines and oil loading terminal, production of oil, natural gas and gas condensate.

Location:
West Anabar license area, Anabar district, Republic of Sakha (Yakutia), Russia.

Markets:
Commercial exports of oil and gas to Europe, countries of the Asia-Pacific region, as well as the domestic market of the Russian Federation.

Annual production volume:
Oil - 12 million tons, gas - 10 billion m3, gas condensate - 114 thousand tons.

Project Duration:
Output to design capacity - 5 years from the beginning of the geological exploration.

The total cost of the project:
₽ 364,7 billion
